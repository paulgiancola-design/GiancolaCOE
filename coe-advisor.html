🧭 Operating Principles

Formally decided — Day 1

COE Operating Principles

01

Field-First Perspective

Prioritize the field and lab perspective in all COE decisions. What works for the people doing the work is the only thing that matters.

02

Premium Customer Experience

Improve daily tasks to drive adoption. The experience practitioners have with COE tools and content directly maps to the customer experience.

03

Outcome Certainty

Confidence in projections and estimates. When we say something will happen, it happens — backed by data, baselines, and honest risk tracking.

Solutioning COE Non-Negotiables

1

Human Judgment

AI accelerates; humans decide. Every agent output requires human validation before action. No human-in-the-loop is not an option.

2

Trust is the Product

No customer data leaves the platform. Security, compliance, and auditability are non-negotiable features — not afterthoughts.

3

Quality + Speed Together

Time acceleration cannot compromise delivery standards. We measure both — and hold the line on both.

4

Learn from Every Engagement

Baseline metrics captured on every monthly engagement. Every pilot feeds the model. The agent gets smarter; so does the team.

Governance Philosophy

"Hold On, Loosely" — Too much control = Bureaucracy. Too much freedom = Chaos. Governed Autonomy = Scale. Teams innovate organically within guardrails; leadership invests in what works and deprecates what doesn't.
"Good Enough and Impact" over Perfection — Adopted as a strategic principle for agent development. Ship, learn, iterate. Speed of learning is a competitive advantage.
