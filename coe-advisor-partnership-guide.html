? What is the COE Advisor?

Your dedicated COE Solutioning Advisor is an embedded field partner — a senior practitioner focused on one Operating Unit who bridges global scoping standards with the realities of your pipeline. The Advisor role exists because strategy only moves deals if it lives in the field, not in a slide deck.

Think of this as having a specialist in your corner who brings the full weight of cross-cloud, cross-OU pattern intelligence to your most complex moments.

✓ What We Do Together

Shape complex opportunities early Pressure-test scope, surface risk, and align delivery assumptions before they become problems.
Strengthen commercial architecture Connect deal structure to margin predictability and package offerings in ways that reduce exposure.
Accelerate solution and delivery design Apply proven delivery patterns so your teams aren't building from scratch on every engagement.
Activate practice and domain depth Bring in the right specialty overlay at the right moment, not as a late escalation.
Build agentic readiness Help your teams scope, price, and position AI and Agentic solutions with confidence.
Return intelligence upward Your frontline patterns and friction points inform how the COE evolves global standards.

✕ What This Is Not

This is not an audit. The Advisor is not here to review your deals for compliance, flag your team for non-standard scoping, or report back to leadership on what you're doing wrong. There is no scorecard. The engagement works best when it's candid — which means the value you get is directly proportional to how openly you bring your real challenges into the room.
