🚦 Rules of the Road

  • Be Present, Bring Your AI — Laptops open for the right reasons. Use Claude, Slackbot, and AI tools to brainstorm and go deeper in real time.
  • Build on the Ideas of Others — "Yes, and" beats "yes, but." Every idea is a starting point — make it better together.
  • Go for Quantity — Don't filter yourself. Get ideas out first, then refine. Volume leads to breakthroughs.
  • Parking Lot — Great idea but off-agenda? It goes on the car park — nothing gets lost.
  • Respect the Clock — Start on time, end on time, breaks are real.
  • No Passengers — Everyone has a role. Share your point of view. Leave with a commitment. This isn't a duck boat tour.

Bureaucracy Busters Program

Nicholas Mannella shared a rundown of the FY27 Lean Agentic Enterprise initiative run by the Office of the COFO (Robin Washington):

  • Employees submit ideas to simplify processes, cut red tape, and remove friction
